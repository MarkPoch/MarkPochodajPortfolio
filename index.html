All About Me

Hello there! I'm Mark Pochodaj, a 26-year-old from Georgetown, Ontario. Ever since I can remember, I've had an insatiable curiosity to understand the outcomes of problems. This innate desire to uncover answers has led me to my passion for data analysis.
